2023 Competition

Carbon Fiber Innovation & 5th Place Overall

Overview

In 2023, Purdue SAE Aero achieved remarkable success at the SAE Aero Design competition, placing 5th overall in the Regular Class. This achievement was the result of months of dedicated work, innovative design, and cutting-edge carbon fiber construction techniques.

Key Achievements

5th Place Overall - Regular Class

Our team secured 5th place out of 40+ competing teams from universities around the world.

Carbon Fiber Design Excellence

Utilized advanced carbon fiber composite materials to create a lightweight yet robust airframe, reducing weight by 25% compared to previous designs.

Payload Capacity

Successfully carried a payload of 18 lbs while maintaining stable flight characteristics.

Flight Performance

Completed all required flight missions with perfect scores in takeoff and landing precision.

Technical Specifications

  • Wingspan: 8.5 feet
  • Empty Weight: 6.2 lbs
  • Max Payload: 18 lbs
  • Propulsion: Electric motor with custom prop
  • Materials: Carbon fiber composite fuselage and wings
  • Flight Time: 12 minutes on single battery

Team Reflections

The 2023 competition was a testament to our team's dedication and innovation. The decision to move to carbon fiber construction proved to be the right choice, providing us with the strength-to-weight ratio needed to compete at the highest level. Our team learned valuable lessons about advanced manufacturing techniques and aerodynamic optimization that will carry forward to future competitions.
