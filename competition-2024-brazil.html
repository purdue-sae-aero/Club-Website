2024 Brazil Competition

Competing Internationally on the Global Stage

Overview

The 2024 SAE Aero Design Brazil competition was an exciting opportunity for our team to compete on the international stage. Representing Purdue University in Brazil, we showcased our engineering excellence and collaborated with teams from around the world, gaining invaluable global competition experience.

Key Achievements

Best Technical Presentation Award

Our team delivered an outstanding technical presentation that clearly communicated our design methodology, analysis, and testing approach to the judges.

Best Design Report Recognition

The design report was recognized as the best among all competitors for its thoroughness, clarity, and technical depth in aerodynamic analysis and structural calculations.

Innovative Design Approach

Implemented a modular wing design that allowed for easy transport and quick assembly at the competition site.

CAD & Analysis Excellence

Developed comprehensive CAD models and performed extensive CFD analysis that was highlighted as exemplary by competition judges.

Technical Specifications

  • Wingspan: 8.8 feet
  • Empty Weight: 7.3 lbs
  • Max Payload: 12 lbs
  • Propulsion: Electric motor with folding propeller
  • Materials: Foam core with fiberglass skin
  • Modular Design: 3-piece wing for transport

Team Reflections

The 2021 competition validated our team's strong foundation in engineering fundamentals. The recognition for our technical documentation and presentation demonstrated that successful competition performance extends beyond just flight performance. The analytical rigor and documentation practices established in 2021 became cornerstones of our team culture, contributing to our subsequent successes in 2022 and 2023.
