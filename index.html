Purdue SAE Aero

Engineering Excellence in Aerospace

Our Mission

Purdue SAE Aero is dedicated to advancing aerospace engineering through innovative design, hands-on experience, and competitive excellence. Our team focuses on developing cutting-edge aircraft solutions that push the boundaries of what's possible in aerospace technology.

We participate in the SAE Aero Design competitions, where students design, build, and fly remote-controlled aircraft to compete in various challenges. This provides our members with invaluable real-world engineering experience.

Our Goals

  • Design and build competitive aircraft for SAE competitions
  • Provide hands-on engineering experience to students
  • Foster collaboration between different engineering disciplines
  • Promote aerospace education and innovation at Purdue
  • Develop the next generation of aerospace engineers

Purdue SAE Aero has a rich history of participating in the Society of Automotive Engineers' Aero Design competitions. Our team has consistently demonstrated excellence in aircraft design, manufacturing, and flight performance.

Our Plan

Competition Strategy

Our plan for upcoming SAE Aero Design competitions focuses on innovative design, rigorous testing, and continuous improvement. We're committed to pushing the boundaries of aircraft performance while maintaining reliability and efficiency.

Development Timeline

Phase 1: Design & Analysis

Conduct aerodynamic analysis, structural simulations, and weight optimization studies to create the optimal aircraft design.

Phase 2: Prototype Construction

Build initial prototypes using advanced manufacturing techniques and lightweight materials to validate our design concepts.

Phase 3: Testing & Refinement

Perform extensive flight testing, gather performance data, and refine the design based on real-world results.

Phase 4: Competition Preparation

Final assembly, team training, documentation, and logistics preparation for competition day.
