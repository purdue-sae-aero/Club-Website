2022 Competition

Mission Success & 15 lbs Payload Capacity

Overview

The 2022 SAE Aero Design competition marked a significant milestone for Purdue SAE Aero. Our team successfully completed all flight missions with a robust design capable of carrying a 15 lb payload. This competition focused on reliability and consistent performance across multiple flight scenarios.

Key Achievements

All Flight Missions Completed

Successfully completed all required flight missions including payload delivery, timed laps, and precision landing challenges.

15 lbs Payload Capacity

Designed and built an aircraft capable of carrying a 15 lb payload while maintaining stable flight characteristics and maneuverability.

Reliability Award Recognition

Received special recognition for consistent performance across all flight attempts with zero mission failures.

Structural Innovation

Implemented a reinforced wing spar design that provided excellent strength while minimizing weight increase.

Technical Specifications

  • Wingspan: 9.2 feet
  • Empty Weight: 8.5 lbs
  • Max Payload: 15 lbs
  • Propulsion: Brushless electric motor
  • Materials: Balsa wood and fiberglass composite
  • Flight Time: 15 minutes on single battery

Team Reflections

The 2022 competition taught our team the importance of reliability and thorough testing. While we didn't achieve the highest placement, successfully completing all missions demonstrated our commitment to quality engineering and careful preparation. The lessons learned about structural design and payload management directly influenced our successful 2023 carbon fiber design.
