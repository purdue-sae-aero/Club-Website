Our Plan

Strategy for Success

Competition Strategy

Our plan for upcoming SAE Aero Design competitions focuses on innovative design, rigorous testing, and continuous improvement. We're committed to pushing the boundaries of aircraft performance while maintaining reliability and efficiency.

Development Timeline

Phase 1: Design & Analysis

Conduct aerodynamic analysis, structural simulations, and weight optimization studies to create the optimal aircraft design.

Phase 2: Prototype Construction

Build initial prototypes using advanced manufacturing techniques and lightweight materials to validate our design concepts.

Phase 3: Testing & Refinement

Perform extensive flight testing, gather performance data, and refine the design based on real-world results.

Phase 4: Competition Preparation

Final assembly, team training, documentation, and logistics preparation for competition day.

Key Focus Areas

  • Advanced aerodynamic optimization
  • Lightweight composite structures
  • Efficient propulsion systems
  • Robust control systems
  • Comprehensive testing protocols
